Recently my TPLink router at my place got fried because of a faulty power connection in my switch board. I got a new one and obviously tore apart the old one to see what's inside. That was the first time I ever saw inside a router/access point system, so it was quite interesting what's inside that plastic box. Once I opened it there were three wires each entering into one of the three antennas. Out of three, one had this removable pin thing and other two were soldered. After seperating the board from the antenna wires, I was finally able to look at each part on the board.
